Technically, I graduated from the Eller College of Business, but no one knows what I mean when I say that.

Upon graduation I learned immediately that I had chosen wrong. I bounced from job to job where I

I was contacted by a company called Cognixia to sponsor me through the bootcamp and help me find a career afterwards.
